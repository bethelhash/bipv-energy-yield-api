## What It Calculates

| Calculation | Model | Source |
|------------|-------|--------|
| Irradiance on tilted surface | HDKR model | Reindl, Beckman & Duffie (1990) Solar Energy 45(5):299–305 |
| Diffuse fraction | Erbs correlation | Erbs, Klein & Duffie (1982) Solar Energy 28(4):309–317 |
| Cell temperature | Sandia model | King et al. (2004) SAND2004-3535 |
| BIPV facade validation | Ross + Sandia | Martín-Chivelet et al. (2022) Sustainability 14(3):1500 |
| BIPV integration losses | IEA PVPS Task 15 | IEA PVPS Task 15 Report (2018) |
| Energy yield & PR | IEC standard | IEC 61724-1:2021 |
| LCOE | IRENA method | IRENA (2023) Renewable Power Generation Costs |
| Degradation | Jordan & Kurtz | Progress in Photovoltaics 21(1):12–29 (2013) |
| CO₂ intensity | IEA database | IEA (2023) CO₂ Emissions from Fuel Combustion |
